How to construct a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich.

Penut butter and Jelly sandwiches have been the main source of food for humans for thousands of years. There are many variations of it, with different toppings and jellys. Today I am going to show you how to construct a standard Peanut butter and Grape Jelly sandwich.

Supplies:

  • Bread
  • butter knife
  • spoon
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Step 1:

Bread Place your two slices of bread opposite eachother on a plate.

Step 2:

Use your butter knife to scoop out a generous portion of peanut butter. The brand of peanut butter that you use is your decisions (i prefer Peter Pan Peanut Butter).

Step 3:

Spreading Once you have the peanut on your knife, begin to spread it over the bread.

Step 4:

Scoop a spoonful of Jelly out of your jar and and place it on top of your peanut butter spread.

Step 5:

Spread the jelly evenlly scross the peanut butter. Make sure to get rid of any clumps!

Step 6:

Unite the two halves

Step 7:

Enjoy! Now that you have completed your fantastic creation, it is time to reap the spoils! take a big old bite out of your sandwich and try not to choke on the peanut butter.
