How to construct a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich.

Supplies:

  • Bread
  • butter knife
  • spoon

  • Jelly

Step 1:

Step 2:

Peanut Butter Use your butter knife to scoop out a generous portion of peanut butter. The brand of peanut butter that you use is your decisions (i prefer Peter Pan Peanut Butter). Once you have the peanut on your knife, begin to spread it over the bread.
